Code of Conduct

Server Rules

To maintain a fair, engaging, and friendly environment, all players are required to follow our server rules. Violations may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans.

01

No Griefing or Stealing

Respect other players' hard work. Do not destroy, modify, or steal from builds, chests, or farms that do not belong to you, unless explicitly permitted by the owner.

02

No Hacking or Cheating

The use of hacked clients, mods that provide unfair advantages (like X-Ray, fly, speed, auto-clickers), or exploitation of game bugs and dupes is strictly prohibited. Minimap and performance mods are allowed.

03

Be Respectful in Chat

Keep the community positive. Do not engage in harassment, hate speech, toxic behavior, excessive spamming, or advertising of other Minecraft servers in the chat.

04

PVP Guidelines

Consensual PVP and duels are encouraged! However, random player killing (Rk), spawn-trapping, or constantly targeting a specific player to ruin their experience is not allowed.

05

Lag Prevention

Keep redstone contraptions and mob farms optimized. Build shut-off switches for high-performance builds. Creating intentional lag machines or overloading the server will result in an immediate ban.

06

Report Exploits

If you discover an exploit, dupe glitch, or server bug, report it immediately to the server owners (arshh or akshansh). Utilizing exploits for personal gain instead of reporting them is a bannable offense.
